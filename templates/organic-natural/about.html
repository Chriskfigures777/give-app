Who We Are

Our Roots

For over 25 years, Creation Church has been rooted in scripture and committed to nurturing a community grounded in God's creation and care.

Our Story

Connecting People to God & Each Other

Creation Church was founded in 1999 by Pastor David Mercer and a small group of passionate believers who wanted to create a church that felt less like an institution and more like a family. What began as a gathering of twelve families in a school gymnasium has grown into a thriving community of over three thousand members.

Our vision is simple: love God, love people, and serve the world. We cultivate an atmosphere of organic growth, where faith can take root and flourish naturally ‚Äî inspired by the beauty of God's creation all around us.
