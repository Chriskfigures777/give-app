Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit to The Cathedral.

What should I wear?

Come as you are! You'll see everything from suits and dresses to jeans and sneakers. We care far more about your presence than your wardrobe. The most important thing is that you feel comfortable.

How long is the service?

Our traditional service runs about 60 minutes and our contemporary service is approximately 75 minutes. Both include worship music, a message, and time for prayer and communion on designated Sundays.

Is there programming for my kids?

Absolutely! We offer age-appropriate programming for children from birth through 5th grade during every Sunday service. Our trained volunteers and background-checked staff create a safe, fun, and faith-filled experience. Check-in opens 15 minutes before each service.

Where do I park?

We have a large main parking lot and a covered parking garage, both accessible from Cathedral Parkway. First-time visitor spots are marked near the main entrance. Our parking team will help guide you in.

Will I be singled out as a visitor?

Never. We want you to feel welcome without any pressure. We do have an optional welcome card you can fill out at your seat if you'd like to receive more information, but it's entirely up to you. No standing up or being called out ‚Äî we promise.
