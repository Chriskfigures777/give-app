About Us

A Legacy of Faith & Beauty

"For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them."

For over a century, The Cathedral has been a beacon of hope in our city. Our doors are open to all who seek meaning, community, and a deeper relationship with God.

Whether you're a lifelong believer or just beginning to explore your faith, you'll find a warm welcome and a place to belong.
