Our Story

A Place of Grace & Reverence

“For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them.”

For over two decades, our congregation has been a beacon of hope in the heart of the community. We believe in the transformative power of worship, the strength of fellowship, and the quiet beauty of a life lived in service to others.

Our doors are open to everyone—whether you’re seeking a spiritual home, a place to ask hard questions, or simply a community that cares. Come as you are, and discover a church family that walks alongside you.
