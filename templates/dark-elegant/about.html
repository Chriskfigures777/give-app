Our Story

Our Heritage

Founded in 1892, The Cathedral has stood as a pillar of faith, beauty, and community for over a century in the heart of our city.

Who We Are

Built on Timeless Truth

The Cathedral was established by a small group of believers who envisioned a sacred space where reverent worship and genuine community would flourish for generations.

Over the decades, our congregation has grown from a handful of families to a vibrant community of thousands ‚Äî united by our commitment to Scripture, service, and the transformative power of God's love.
