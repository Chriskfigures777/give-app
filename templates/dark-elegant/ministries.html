Small Groups

Life Is Better Together

Church isn't just what happens on Sunday morning. Our small groups meet throughout the week in homes, coffee shops, and parks across the city — creating spaces where real relationships form and authentic faith grows.

Each group is led by trained leaders who facilitate discussion, prayer, and mutual support. Whether you're brand new to faith or have been walking with Jesus for decades, there's a group that feels like home.
