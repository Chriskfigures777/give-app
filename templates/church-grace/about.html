Our Story

Built on Faith, United in Love

Grace Community Church was founded in 1962 by a small group of families who shared a vision for a church that would be a beacon of hope in our community. What began as gatherings in a living room has grown into a vibrant congregation of hundreds.

Over six decades, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to biblical teaching, authentic worship, and compassionate service. Our doors are open to everyone‚Äîno matter where you are on your faith journey.

Today, we continue to build on the foundation laid by our founders, embracing new generations while honoring the timeless truths of Scripture.

Our Journey

A Legacy of Grace

1962

The Beginning

Twelve families gathered in a living room with a dream to plant a gospel-centered church in Springfield.

1975

First Building

After years of faithful growth, the congregation moved into its first dedicated sanctuary on Grace Avenue.

1998

Community Center

The Grace Community Center was built, expanding our ability to serve families and host outreach programs.

2015

Modern Expansion

A new worship center and youth facility were completed, welcoming even more families into our church home.

Today

Growing Forward

With hundreds of active members and dozens of ministries, Grace Community continues to impact lives for Christ.
