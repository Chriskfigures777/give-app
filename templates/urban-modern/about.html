About City Church

For over 25 years, we've been rooted in scripture and committed to serving our city with love, innovation, and purpose.

Our Story

Connecting People to God & Each Other

City Church was founded in 1999 by Pastor David Mercer and a small group of passionate believers who wanted to create a church that felt less like an institution and more like a family. What began as a gathering of twelve families in a school gymnasium has grown into a thriving community of over three thousand members passionate about making faith relevant in an urban context.

Our vision is simple: love God, love people, and serve the world. Everyone ‚Äî regardless of background, past, or circumstance ‚Äî deserves to experience the transforming love of Jesus Christ. We bring a modern, forward-thinking approach to ministry while staying grounded in timeless biblical truth.
