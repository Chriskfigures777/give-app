For over 25 years, our church has been rooted in scripture and committed to serving our city with love, innovation, and purpose.

Our Mission

Connecting People to God & Each Other

Our church was founded in 1999 by Pastor David Mercer and a small group of passionate believers who wanted to create a church that felt less like an institution and more like a family. What began as a gathering of twelve families in a school gymnasium has grown into a thriving community of over three thousand members who are passionate about making faith relevant in an urban context.

Our vision is simple: love God, love people, and serve the world. Everyone ‚Äî regardless of background, past, or circumstance ‚Äî deserves to experience the transforming love of Jesus Christ. We bring a modern, forward-thinking approach to ministry while staying grounded in timeless biblical truth.

Our History

A Timeline of Faith

1999

The Beginning

Pastor David Mercer and 12 founding families held the first service in a local school gymnasium, united by a shared vision of building a Christ-centered community.

2003

Our First Building

After four years of faithful growth, the church moved into its first permanent home on Faith Avenue with 300 dedicated members.

2009

Launching Missions

Sent our first mission team to Kenya, beginning a global outreach ministry that now spans four continents and continues to this day.

2015

Campus Expansion

Broke ground on the Grace Center, a 40,000 sq ft community hub designed for worship, education, youth programs, and community service.

2024

25 Years & Growing

Celebrating a quarter century of ministry with 3,200+ members, 47 active ministries, and 12 global mission partnerships.
