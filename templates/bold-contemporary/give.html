Every Gift Matters

When you give to our church, you are investing in something bigger than a building or a program. You are investing in people. Your generosity funds youth programs that shape the next generation, outreach ministries that serve the vulnerable, and mission trips that carry the gospel to the ends of the earth.

We believe in financial transparency and faithful stewardship. Every dollar is carefully managed and directed toward kingdom impact. Our annual financial reports are available to all members.

Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver. — 2 Corinthians 9:7

Whether it is a one-time gift or a recurring commitment, your generosity makes a lasting difference in our community and beyond.
