What Your First Visit Looks Like

We know visiting a new church can feel overwhelming. Here's what you need to know so you can walk in with confidence.

★

What to Expect

From the moment you pull into the parking lot, you'll be greeted by a friendly team ready to help you find your way. Enjoy contemporary worship, a relevant message, and an atmosphere where everyone is welcome — no matter where you are on your faith journey. Our services are about 75 minutes, and the dress code is simple: come as you are.

⏲

Service Times

We offer multiple services throughout the week so you can find a time that fits your schedule. Sunday mornings at 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM are our main gatherings, and Wednesday evening at 7:00 PM is our midweek experience. Each service features the same powerful worship and teaching, so choose the time that works best for you and your family.

⛪

Location

We're located in the heart of the city at 456 Main Street, Metro City, ST 67890. Our campus is easy to find with clearly marked entrances and plenty of free parking. Look for the greeters in red shirts — they'll point you to the welcome center, the auditorium, and our children's area. We can't wait to see you walk through those doors.

Common Questions

We get it — visiting somewhere new raises questions. Here are the ones we hear most often.

Q1

What Should I Wear?

There is no dress code at Your Church. You'll see everything from jeans and sneakers to suits and ties. We care more about what's in your heart than what's on your back. Wear whatever makes you feel comfortable and come as you are — seriously.

Q2

Where Do I Park?

We have two spacious parking lots with clearly marked first-time visitor spots right near the main entrance. Our parking team (look for the red vests) will guide you in and help you find the best spot. Overflow parking is available on Oak Avenue with a short, easy walk to the building.

Q3

What About My Kids?

Your kids are going to love it here. We offer age-appropriate programming for nursery through 5th grade during every service. Our children's ministry uses a secure check-in system, and all volunteers are background-checked. Kids experience interactive worship, Bible stories, games, and small groups designed to make faith real and fun.

Q4

How Long Is the Service?

Our services typically last about 75 minutes. You'll experience around 25 minutes of live worship music followed by a 35–40 minute message from one of our pastors. We wrap up with a closing moment and an opportunity to connect. It goes by faster than you'd think — we promise you won't be checking your watch.

We Can't Wait to Meet You

Your next step could change everything. Whether you're searching for answers, looking for community, or just curious — there's a seat here with your name on it.
