Common Questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

What should I wear?

+

Come as you are. You will see everything from jeans and sneakers to suits. We care more about your heart than your wardrobe. Wear whatever makes you comfortable.

What about my kids?

+

We have safe, fun, and age-appropriate programs for children from birth through 5th grade during every service. Our team uses a secure check-in system so you can worship with peace of mind.

How long is the service?

+

Our Sunday services are approximately 75 minutes and include live worship music, a relevant message from the Bible, and opportunities to connect with others.

Where do I park?

+

Free parking is available in our main lot and the adjacent parking garage. First-time guest parking spots are reserved near the main entrance. Our parking team will help direct you.

Is the building accessible?

+

Yes. Our facility is fully ADA accessible with ramps, elevators, and designated seating. We also offer assisted listening devices and large-print bulletins upon request.
