Ways to Serve & Grow

From worship to outreach, there is a place for everyone. Explore our ministries and find the one that stirs your heart.

Worship

Our worship team leads the congregation into powerful encounters every week through music, vocals, and creative arts. Whether you play an instrument, sing, or run sound — there is a spot for you on this team.

Youth

Our middle and high school ministry creates a space where students can build authentic friendships, grow deeper in faith, and discover who God created them to be through dynamic teaching and real community.

Learn More →

Children

From nursery through fifth grade, our kids experience age-appropriate worship, Bible teaching, and hands-on activities that lay a foundation of faith they will carry for a lifetime.

Learn More →

Small Groups

Life is better together. Our small groups meet weekly in homes across the city for Bible study, prayer, and authentic fellowship. This is where real relationships are built and faith gets personal.

Outreach

We believe the church exists for those outside its walls. Through food drives, community events, global missions, and neighborhood partnerships, we bring the love of Christ to those who need it most.

Men's & Women's

Life-Changing Small Groups

Don't just attend church — be the church. Our groups meet in living rooms, coffee shops, and parks all across the city.

Community

Real People, Real Faith

Our small groups are the heartbeat of our church. Every week, hundreds of people gather in homes to study the Bible, share life's highs and lows, and pray for one another. It's where Sunday's message becomes Monday's reality.

  • Over 50 active groups across the metro area
  • Groups for every season of life — singles, couples, parents, seniors
  • New group semesters launch every January and September

Growth

Go Deeper Together

Small groups aren't just about socializing — they're about transformation. Each semester follows a curated curriculum designed to help you understand Scripture, wrestle with tough questions, and apply truth to everyday life.

  • Verse-by-verse studies and topical deep dives
  • Trained group leaders who walk alongside you
  • Resources and devotionals provided each week

Impact

Serve Your Neighborhood

Every small group is encouraged to adopt a service project each semester. From packing meals at the food bank to mentoring students at a local school, our groups are the hands and feet of Jesus right where they live.

  • Quarterly neighborhood serve days
  • Partnerships with local nonprofits and schools
  • Over 2,000 volunteer hours logged last year
