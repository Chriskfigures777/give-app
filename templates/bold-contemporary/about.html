Our Story

Who We Are

A Christ-centered community rooted in bold faith, authentic relationships, and an unstoppable mission to make disciples.

Est. 2005

From a Living Room to a Movement

Faith Church started with twelve people gathered in a living room, united by a single conviction: the gospel changes everything. What began as a small Bible study quickly grew into a thriving community of believers.

We exist to glorify God by making disciples who worship boldly, connect deeply, and serve relentlessly.

Today, we are a multi-generational church reaching thousands across our city and beyond. But our heart remains the same — to see lives transformed by the power of Jesus Christ.

We believe the local church is the hope of the world. Every program, every ministry, and every gathering exists for one purpose: to point people to Jesus.
