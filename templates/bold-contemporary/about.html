Our Mission

Why We Exist

Your Church was founded on a simple but powerful conviction: that every person matters to God and deserves a place where they can encounter His love, grow in their faith, and discover their purpose. We believe the local church is the hope of the world, and we take that calling seriously.

From our very first gathering of twelve people in a living room, we have been driven by the desire to create a community where authenticity is celebrated, where questions are welcome, and where the message of Jesus is presented in a way that is both uncompromising and accessible. We are not interested in religion for the sake of tradition. We are interested in transformation.

Today, our mission reaches far beyond our walls. Through local outreach initiatives, global missions partnerships, and a growing digital presence, we are committed to bringing hope, healing, and the tangible love of Christ to every corner of our community and the world.

What We Believe

Anchored in Truth

At the heart of everything we do is the unchanging Word of God. We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative guide for life and faith, and we are committed to teaching it with clarity, conviction, and compassion. Our beliefs are not just theological positions written on a page; they shape the way we live, the way we love, and the way we serve.

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully divine and fully human, who lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, and rose again on the third day. Salvation is a free gift, received through faith alone, not by any works of our own.

We believe the Holy Spirit empowers believers to live with boldness and purpose. We believe in the power of prayer, the importance of community, the sacredness of worship, and the urgency of sharing the Gospel with a world that desperately needs hope. These truths are the bedrock on which our church stands.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a thriving community — every step guided by faith

1998 — The Beginning

A Living Room, A Dream

What started as a small group of twelve friends meeting in a living room was the spark that would ignite a movement. Pastor James and Maria Rivera felt called to plant a church that would be radically different — real, raw, and relevant. With nothing but faith and a folding table, the first seeds were planted.

2003 — Growth

First Permanent Home

After five years of meeting in rented spaces, school gyms, and community centers, we moved into our first permanent building. Attendance had grown to over 300 people, and the need for a dedicated space for worship, children's ministry, and community outreach was undeniable. It was a milestone that tested and strengthened our faith.

2010 — Expansion

Launching Global Missions

With a heart that could not be contained by city limits, we sent our first missions team to Central America. That initial trip opened the door to partnerships across three continents. Today, our global missions program supports church plants, orphanages, clean water projects, and educational initiatives in over ten countries.

2016 — New Campus

Building for the Future

Fueled by the generosity of our congregation, we completed construction on our current 45,000-square-foot campus. The new facility includes a 1,200-seat auditorium, a state-of-the-art children's wing, a community café, and dedicated spaces for youth and young adults. It was a bold vision made possible by a community that gives boldly.

2020 — Resilience

Church Without Walls

When the world shut down, our church opened up — online. We launched a robust digital ministry, reaching thousands of people across the globe with livestream services, virtual small groups, and an online prayer team. What could have been our greatest setback became our greatest breakthrough, proving that the church is not a building but a people.

2024 — Today

The Best Is Yet to Come

With over 2,500 members, 30+ active ministries, and a presence in communities around the world, we are just getting started. Our vision is bigger than ever: to see every life in our city and beyond transformed by the love of Jesus Christ. We are investing in the next generation, expanding our outreach, and believing God for the impossible.

What Defines Us

Four pillars that shape every ministry, every decision, and every gathering

✠

Biblical Authority

We hold the Bible as our ultimate source of truth and wisdom. Every sermon, every program, and every decision is filtered through the lens of Scripture. We are committed to teaching God's Word faithfully, equipping every believer to know it, love it, and live it out in their daily lives.

♥

Authentic Community

We were never meant to do life alone. At Your Church, real relationships are at the center of everything. Through small groups, serve teams, and intentional gatherings, we create spaces where people can be known, loved, and challenged to grow in their faith alongside one another.

★

Passionate Worship

Worship is more than a song — it is a lifestyle. We gather each week to encounter the living God through bold, Spirit-led worship that moves hearts and transforms lives. Our creative teams pour their gifts into creating an atmosphere where every person can connect with God in a profound way.

✈

Radical Generosity

We believe generosity changes everything — the giver, the receiver, and the world around them. Whether it is through tithing, volunteering, or global missions, we are a church that gives boldly, loves extravagantly, and serves sacrificially because that is what Jesus modeled for us.
