Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We've answered the most common questions visitors ask. Still have questions? Reach out!

What should I wear? +

Come as you are! You'll see everything from jeans and t-shirts to dresses and suits. We care more about you being here than what you're wearing.

Where do I park? +

We have a large free parking lot right next to the building. First-time visitors can use the designated visitor spots near the main entrance. Our parking team will help guide you.

What about my kids? +

We have age-appropriate programs for kids from nursery through 5th grade during every service. Our volunteers are background-checked and trained to create a safe, fun environment. Check-in is easy — just stop by our kids' area when you arrive.

How long is a typical service? +

Our services typically last about 75 minutes and include worship music, a message, and time for prayer. We always finish on time so you can plan your day accordingly.

Is there anything for teens? +

Absolutely! Our youth ministry meets on Fridays at 6:30 PM and during Sunday services. It's a great place for students to connect, have fun, and grow in their faith with other teens.
