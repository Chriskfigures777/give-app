Who We Are

Our Story, Mission & Values

We're a community of believers passionate about knowing God, growing together, and serving the world around us.

Our Mission

For over 25 years, we've been committed to one simple goal: helping people discover and follow Jesus Christ. We believe faith is not just about belief—it's about transformation, community, and living out God's love in practical ways.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or have walked with God for decades, you'll find a welcoming community here where questions are encouraged and grace abounds.

What We Believe

We are rooted in historic Christian faith. We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word, Jesus Christ is the Way, the Truth, and the Life, and the Holy Spirit empowers us to live transformed lives.

Our worship is contemporary yet reverent, our teaching is biblical and practical, and our community is diverse and welcoming. We're committed to loving God, loving people, and serving the world.
