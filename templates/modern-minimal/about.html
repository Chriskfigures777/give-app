Who We Are

About Our Church

Rooted in faith, growing in love, and reaching our community with the hope of Jesus Christ.

Our Story

Built on Faith,
Driven by Love

What began as a small gathering in a living room over 25 years ago has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational community of believers. Through every season, our mission has remained the same: to know God, to grow together, and to make Him known.

Today, we're a church of hundreds of families united by a common purpose. We gather each week not out of obligation, but out of a genuine desire to worship, learn, and serve alongside one another.

Our doors are open to everyone — no matter where you are on your faith journey. You belong here.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

These values shape everything we do — from how we worship to how we serve our neighbors.

☯

Authentic Worship

We come as we are, offering genuine praise and creating space for God to move in our hearts.

♥

Radical Love

We love without conditions, welcoming everyone and reflecting the unconditional love of Christ.

★

Biblical Truth

God's Word is our foundation. We teach it faithfully and apply it practically to everyday life.

✦

Generous Living

We give freely of our time, talents, and resources to bless others and advance God's Kingdom.
