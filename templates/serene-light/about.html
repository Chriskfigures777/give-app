About Our Church

For over 25 years, Serenity Church has been rooted in scripture and committed to serving our community with love, grace, and purpose.

Our Story

Connecting People to God & Each Other

Serenity Church was founded in 1999 by Pastor David Mercer and a small group of passionate believers who wanted to create a church that felt less like an institution and more like a family. What began as a gathering of twelve families in a school gymnasium has grown into a thriving community of over three thousand members.

Our vision is simple: love God, love people, and serve the world. Everyone ‚Äî regardless of background, past, or circumstance ‚Äî deserves to experience the transforming love of Jesus Christ. We are committed to creating a space where people can encounter God's grace in a real and lasting way.
