Welcome to Our Church Family

"The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want. He maketh me to lie down in green pastures."

For over 25 years, we've been a beacon of hope and faithfulness in our community. Whether you're new to faith or have walked with Christ for years, you'll find a warm welcome here.

We believe in the power of scripture, the importance of prayer, and the transforming love of Jesus Christ. Come experience authentic worship and biblical teaching in a community that cares.
