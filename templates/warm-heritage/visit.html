Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Come as you are! You’ll see everything from suits to jeans. We want you to feel comfortable and focus on worship, not your wardrobe.

Do you have programs for children?

Yes! We offer age-appropriate programs for nursery through 5th grade during all Sunday services. Check in at our secure Welcome Desk when you arrive.

Where should I park?

We have a large parking lot with designated first-time visitor spots near the main entrance. Greeters will be available to help direct you.

How long are your services?

Our Sunday services typically last about 75 minutes, including worship music and a message. Wednesday Bible studies are about 60 minutes.

Is the building accessible?

Absolutely. Our building is fully ADA accessible with ramp entrances, elevator access, wheelchair seating, and assisted listening devices available at the Welcome Desk.
