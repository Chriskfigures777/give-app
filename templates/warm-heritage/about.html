Our Story & Heritage

A faithful witness in our community for over 25 years, grounded in Scripture and committed to Christ's mission.

Our Mission

We exist to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and evangelism. For over two decades, we've been faithful to this calling.

Whether you're seeking answers, needing encouragement, or ready to serve, you'll find a spiritual home here where God's Word is honored and His people are loved.

What We Believe

We affirm the historic Christian faith as expressed in the Apostles' Creed. The Bible is our final authority, Jesus Christ is the only way to salvation, and the Holy Spirit empowers believers for godly living.

Our worship honors tradition while engaging contemporary hearts. We're committed to sound doctrine, fervent prayer, and authentic community.
