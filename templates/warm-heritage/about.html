Our Heritage

A faithful witness in our community for over 25 years, grounded in Scripture and committed to Christ’s mission.

Our Story

Heritage Church was founded in 1998 by a small group of families who shared a vision for a church that honored timeless biblical truths while warmly welcoming all who seek God. What began as a living room Bible study has grown into a vibrant community of over 2,800 members.

Through seasons of growth and challenge, our commitment to faithful preaching, authentic worship, and compassionate outreach has never wavered. Today, Heritage Church continues to be a place where tradition and transformation meet.

What We Believe

We affirm the historic Christian faith as expressed in the Apostles’ Creed. The Bible is our final authority, Jesus Christ is the only way to salvation, and the Holy Spirit empowers believers for godly living.

Our worship honors tradition while engaging contemporary hearts. We are committed to sound doctrine, fervent prayer, and authentic community that reflects the love of Christ.
