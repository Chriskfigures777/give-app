Our Story

A Legacy of Faithfulness

Reformed Presbyterian was established in 1892 by a small band of Scottish immigrants who carried with them a deep love for the Reformed faith and a commitment to the Westminster Standards. What began as a handful of families gathering in a farmhouse parlor has, by God's grace, grown into a vibrant congregation of over one thousand members.

Through two World Wars, the Depression, and the sweeping cultural changes of the twentieth century, our church has remained anchored to the unchanging Word of God. We believe that the same gospel that sustained our forebears sustains us today ‚Äî and will sustain generations yet to come.

Our mission is clear: to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through the faithful preaching of the Word, the proper administration of the sacraments, and the diligent exercise of church discipline in love.
