Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What should I wear?

Our congregation wears everything from suits and dresses to jeans and polos. There is no dress code ‚Äî we care about your heart, not your wardrobe. Come as you are.

Do you have programs for children?

Yes! We offer nursery care for infants and toddlers, a children's catechism class for ages 4‚Äì12 during the sermon, and a full Sunday School program at 9:00 AM for all ages.

How long is a typical service?

About 75 minutes. We begin with a call to worship, sing psalms and hymns, hear the reading and preaching of God's Word, celebrate the sacraments as appropriate, and close with a benediction.

What Bible translation do you use?

We primarily use the English Standard Version (ESV) in our preaching and teaching. Pew Bibles are available in every row, and we project the text on screen as well.

Will I be singled out as a visitor?

Never publicly. We want you to feel comfortable, not spotlighted. You may choose to fill out a visitor card, and our greeters are available to answer any questions ‚Äî but there is no pressure at all.
