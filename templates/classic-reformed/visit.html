Frequently Asked

What should I wear?

Come as you are! Our congregation wears everything from jeans and sneakers to dresses and slacks. There's no dress code here ‚Äî we care about your heart, not your wardrobe.

Do you have programs for kids?

Absolutely. We offer age-appropriate programming for infants through 5th grade during every service. Check-in opens 15 minutes before each service at our kids' wing.

How long is a typical service?

About 75 minutes. We start with 20‚Äì25 minutes of worship music, followed by a 35‚Äì40 minute message, and close with a few minutes of response and community announcements.

Is there coffee?

Yes! Our coffee bar opens 30 minutes before each service with complimentary drip coffee, espresso drinks, and pastries. It's the perfect place to ease into your Sunday morning.

Never. We want you to feel comfortable, not spotlighted. You're welcome to be as anonymous or as engaged as you'd like.
